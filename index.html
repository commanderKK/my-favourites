My favourite character

My favourite charactar is hilda from the show hilda


Hilda is a adventertous blue-haired character who stars in her own show and comics. She is a girl who who lives in the city of trollberg.Hilda is the titular protagonist of the award-winning British graphic novel series, the British-Canadian Netflix-exclusive television series, and the novelization of the animated series. She, along with her pet deer-fox Twig, her mother Johanna, and her friends from the city of Trolberg as well as a little elf named Alfur and its surroundings, embarks in a multitude of supernatural adventures, encountering a diverse cast of magical creatures inspired by Scandinavian folklore.
